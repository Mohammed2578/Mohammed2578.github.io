Meet the Frogs

Frogs are incredible amphibians that live on every continent except Antarctica. Their smooth, moist skin helps them breathe through it.

Life Cycle

Frogs begin life as eggs in water, hatch into tadpoles, and grow legs as they transform into adult frogs. This is called metamorphosis.

Behavior

Many frogs are nocturnal. They use sticky tongues to catch insects and call loudly to attract mates.
